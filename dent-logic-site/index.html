FAQ: Paintless Dent Repair

Will PDR affect my paint or carfax?

PDR preserves your OEM paint—no fillers or repainting. Most minor PDR won’t appear on Carfax since it isn’t a collision repair.

How long does a typical repair take?

Small door dings can be 30–90 minutes. Hail repairs vary by severity and can take a day or more.

Can you handle insurance hail claims?

Yes. We work with insurers. We’ll assess damage, provide documentation, and coordinate with your adjuster.

What photos should I send for an estimate?

Take 2–3 angles in bright, indirect light so panel reflections show the dent clearly. Include year/make/model and your zip.
