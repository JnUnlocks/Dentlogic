FAQ: Paintless Dent Repair

Will PDR affect my paint or Carfax?

PDR preserves your OEM paint with no fillers or repainting. Most minor PDR work does not appear as collision repair.

How long does a typical repair take?

Small door dings can take 30-90 minutes. Hail repairs vary by severity and may take a day or more.

Can you handle insurance hail claims?

Yes. We work with insurers and can provide the documentation needed for your adjuster.

What photos should I send for an estimate?

Take 2-3 angles in bright, indirect light so reflections show the dent shape clearly. Include year, make, model, and zip code.
